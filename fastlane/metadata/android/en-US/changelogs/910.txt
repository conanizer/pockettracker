First F-Droid release.

• Sampler (WAV/MP3/FLAC/OGG/Opus/M4A) and SoundFont (SF2) instruments
• Sample editor with slicing, SOLA time-stretch, pitch-shift and offline FX
• Sample-from-video audio extraction
• Per-instrument modulation (AHD/ADSR/LFO), per-track reverb/delay sends
• Master bus OTT/DUST + limiter, WAV mix and stems export
• Themes, theme editor, visualizers; touch layout with device skins
